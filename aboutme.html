About Me
  • I was born in Riverside,CA and have always been fond of creativity. Whether if
    it's through problem solving or making something from scratch it has always
    attracted me.

  • BACKGROUND: I graduated in 2015 with a BA in Philosophy. Initially deciding
    to go to law school, but instantly came to a realization it wasn't something I had
    a passion for. Spent a couple years trying to pursue film until I realized I wanted
    to do something more. Shortly after programming was brought into in my life
    and there was immediate intimidation and love for it, a very odd mix. However,
    I persisted and ended up pursuing my second bachelors degree in Computer
    Science at Oregon State University.

  • PROGRAMMING SKILLS: C++, C, Python, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3

  • INTERESTS: Asides from programming, filming, photography, and traveling
    are on top of the list. I've travelled to 9 countries including Canada, India,
    Tanzania, United Arab Emirates, Spain, Greece, Turkey, Mexico, and Great Britain.
    In terms of entertainment, Mr. Robot is my favorite show and Bohemian Rhapsody
    is my favorite song.

  • PROJECTS: Here are a couple of projects I've worked on. NextWord was
    created in C++ and is a word guessing game where two players must compete to
    guess more correct words than their opponent. Skyrim: Ruins of Alftand is a
    terminal text game created for a class project. It was also written in C++ and was
    picked as one of the best class projects. Click on the projects below to view them
    on GitHub!
