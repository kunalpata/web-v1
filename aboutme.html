About Me
  • My name is Kunal Patadia and I was born in Riverside,CA. I've lived here
    most of my life and have always been fond with creativity. Whether if it's
    through problem solving or making something from scratch it has always
    attracted me.

  • BACKGROUND: I graduated in 2015 with a BA in Philosophy. I initially decided
    I wanted to go to law school, but quickly realized it wasn't something I had a
    passion for. I spent a couple years trying to pursue film until I realized I wanted
    to do something more. I was introduced to programming and immediately
    felt intimidation and love, a very odd mix. However, I persisted and ended up
    pursuing my second bachelors degree in Computer Science at Oregon State
    University.

  • PROGRAMMING SKILLS: C++, C, Python, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3

  • INTERESTS: Asides from programming, I love to travel, film, and photography.
    I've travelled to 9 countries including Canada, India, Tanzania, United Arab Emirates,
    Spain, Greece, Turkey, Mexico, and Great Britain. My favorite show is Mr. Robot
    and my favorite song is Bohemian Rhapsody.
